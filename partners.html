Partners

Effortless ways you can help iPAW throughout the year


Already shop at Amazon.com? Switch to Amazon Smile for the same great products and Amazon will make a donation to support iPAW. Click the image or the link and select us as your charitable organization.


Shop at Kroger? Enroll your Kroger Plus Card into their Community Rewards Program and Kroger will make a financial donation to iPAW. Annually select us as the organization you wish to support. Click the image or the link to enroll.


Walk your dog? WoofTrax donates to the group based on the number of subscribers we have so please share with friends and family who are also walkers. If you don’t have a dog, WoofTrax will lend you their virtual dog, Bonnie!
