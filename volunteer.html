iPAW Volunteer Opportunities

Thank you for your interest in volunteering with iPAW! Volunteers are the heartbeat of our organization and needed in every part of our organization! We want to help you find the best way to use your time and talents to support iPAW’s mission and programs. Whether you want to work longer term with dogs, or help with administrative items, we have opportunities for you to be integral in the life of an animal in need. To get started simply fill out the Volunteer application and submit. You should hear back from us within 48 hours. To help generate ideas, we have listed a job bank below.
