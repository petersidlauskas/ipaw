Foster

Fostering many times is truly the difference between Life and Death!


By opening your home to a foster animal for iPAW, you:
  • Allow us to bring another dog into our program.
  • Get an animal out of or keep it from going to a shelter.
  • Learn more about the animal in a home environment; providing us with invaluable information that helps us find the right home for the animal.
  • Give an animal exposure to potential adopters outside the internet. Many of our fosters have placed their foster dogs with friends, family or people within their communities.

If you are interested in fostering, please fill out and submit the foster application. You should then hear from us within 48 hours.

Adoption Fees

  • The adoption fee is $450. Each dog will be microchipped and registered to the adopter. It will have received its age-appropriate vaccines, tested for heartworm and other parasites, spayed/neutered if old enough, and MDR1 tested if needed.
  • Senior citizens (62+ years), those on disability or fixed income and veterans are eligible for participation in our Paws for Seniors senior-to-senior program. This program offers a couple options tailored at removing any financial barrier so that those who are mentally and physically able to care for a dog can do so without the fear of financial difficulty. whereby the $450 adoption fee is waived.
